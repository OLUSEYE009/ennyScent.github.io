Product Questions

How do I choose the right fragrance?

Choosing a fragrance depends on your personal preferences and the occasion. Consider:

  • Season: Lighter scents for summer, warmer scents for winter
  • Time of day: Fresh scents for daytime, richer scents for evening
  • Personality: Our website includes a fragrance quiz to help you find scents that match your style

You can also order our sample set to try multiple fragrances before committing to a full bottle.

How long does a bottle typically last?

A 100ml bottle used daily (2-3 sprays) typically lasts 6-8 months. Smaller bottles (50ml) last about 3-4 months with the same usage.

To extend your fragrance's lifespan, store it in a cool, dark place away from direct sunlight and heat.

Do you offer sample sizes?

Yes! We offer a discovery set that includes 5ml samples of our most popular fragrances. This is a great way to explore our collection before purchasing full-size bottles.
