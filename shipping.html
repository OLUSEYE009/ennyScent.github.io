Shipping Policy

We strive to process and ship all orders as quickly as possible. Please review the following information about our shipping process.

Processing Time

Orders are typically processed within 1-2 business days (Monday-Friday, excluding holidays). During peak seasons, processing may take slightly longer.

International Shipping

We ship to most countries worldwide. International orders may be subject to customs fees, import duties, and taxes, which are the responsibility of the customer.

Delivery times vary by destination but typically range from 7-21 business days.

Order Tracking

Once your order has shipped, you will receive a confirmation email with tracking information. You can track your package using the provided tracking number.

Return Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your order, we accept returns under the following conditions:

Eligibility

  • Items must be returned within 5 days of delivery
  • Products must be unopened, unused, and in their original packaging
  • Proof of purchase is required

Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:

  • Opened or used perfume bottles
  • Gift cards
  • Final sale items (clearly marked at time of purchase)

Return Process

  1. Contact our customer service at support@ennyscent.com to initiate your return
  2. You will receive a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number
  3. Package the item securely with the RMA number clearly visible
  4. Ship the package to the address provided (customer is responsible for return shipping costs)

Refunds

Once we receive and inspect your return, we will process your refund within 5-7 business days. Refunds will be issued to the original payment method.

Shipping costs are non-refundable. For defective or incorrect items, we will cover return shipping costs.

Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact us immediately at support@ennyscent.com with photos of the damage.

We will arrange for a replacement or refund and provide a prepaid return label if needed.
